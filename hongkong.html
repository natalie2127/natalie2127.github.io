HONG KONG

We welcome over 42 million visitors a year to Hong Kong now so hopefully you can find a reason to visit as well. A trip for 6-8 days is fairly standard, I wouldn't suggest flying all the way from the states for anything fewer than 5-6 days simply due to the very long flights, time required to adjust to the time differences and the expenses. As you have someone to stay with, you will not need to consider the costs of accommodation, which is nice. If you intend to visit Macau as well, I'd suggest a trip of 7-9 days, so that you can spend 2-3 nights there. Generally speaking HK is most famous for dining and shopping but we have a fairly impressive amount of entertainment as well. No, this isn't NYC, London, Paris or Rome but outside that particular group, you will find few major cities that can offer as much to see/do and experience. Plus, Macau is just an hour away by ferry. There are just over 30 World Heritage attractions, an extra 20 museums and some fantastic dining. Oh yes, and the mega hotel/casinos.I hope you will come to HK one day and enjoy HK and maybe meeting me.
